Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Overview

PROJKT ("we", "our", "the app") is a music project management app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we handle it.

What We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you create an account. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email from those providers.
  • Profile data: Username and optional avatar you set during onboarding.
  • Project data: Project names, track titles, notes, and to-do items you create within the app. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Audio files: Audio files you import are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain your account
  • To enable sharing features (when you choose to share a project link)
  • To sync your profile across devices

Data Storage

Your audio files, project metadata, and notes are stored locally on your device. Account authentication is handled by Supabase, which stores your email and profile information securely on their servers. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Authentication and profile storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Apple / Google: If you use social sign-in, their respective privacy policies apply.
  • Expo: App updates and build services. Expo Privacy Policy

Sharing

When you share a project link, the project title and track list metadata are made accessible via that link. You can revoke share links at any time from within the app. Audio files are not shared through share links.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile settings screen within the app. This removes your profile data from our servers. Local data (audio files, projects) can be removed by deleting the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

PROJKT is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@projkt.app.
